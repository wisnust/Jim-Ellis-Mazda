My Mazda App

Manage Your Mazda From The Palm Of Your Hand.

The MyMazda App1 makes your Mazda experience simpler and more convenient than ever. Calibrated to your vehicle’s mileage, MyMazda shows your Mazda’s maintenance schedule and remembers its service history at Mazda Dealers for easy reference.

MyMazda also helps you save with coupons about service offers exclusively for Mazda owners. And with the app, you’ll have the contact information of your service dealer and the nearest Mazda Dealers right at your fingertips, making it fast and easy to schedule a service appointment.

An easy-to-use, interactive guide and quick video demos will help walk you through your vehicle’s features, such as the instrument panel, navigation, entertainment system and more.
